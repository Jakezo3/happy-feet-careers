Who We Are

MORE THAN
A SHOE STORE

Happy Feet is a mission-driven thrift shop that exists to make quality footwear accessible to every member of the Spokane community — students, families, and everyone in between. We source, curate, and resell shoes sustainably.

♻️
Sustainability First

We reduce footwear waste by giving great shoes a second life instead of letting them end up in a landfill.

🤝
Community Roots

We exist to serve Spokane. From student discounts to donation partnerships, we're embedded in this city.

📈
Promote From Within

We look internally first when roles open up. Your loyalty and hard work will be recognized and rewarded.

The Position

RETAIL SALES ASSOCIATE

You're the face of Happy Feet. You'll help customers find the perfect pair, keep the floor running smoothly, and bring energy that keeps people coming back.

This is a role built for people who love connecting with others, can think on their feet (pun intended), and take genuine pride in delivering a great experience. Whether you're a student looking for flexible hours or someone building a retail career — there's a place for you here.

📅 PART-TIME & FULL-TIME
💰 COMPETITIVE PAY
🕐 FLEXIBLE HOURS
01

Customer Connection

Greet every customer, ask the right questions, observe their preferences, and make them feel genuinely welcome the moment they walk in.

02

Product Expert

Recommend and help locate merchandise that matches what each customer actually needs — you become the go-to shoe authority.

03

Drive Sales

Sell merchandise and services while encouraging additional purchases through genuine enthusiasm for the product.

04

Process Payments

Compute sales and process transactions accurately by cash, credit, or other payment methods using our POS system.

05

Answer Everything

Be a reliable source on products, services, policies, and promotions — turning questions into confident purchase decisions.
